I'm Michael Crouse — a small-time property owner, investor, operator, and licensed real estate agent in Southern Illinois.

Over the past 5+ years I've bought, rehabbed, rented, and refinanced small multifamily properties using the BRRRR method — and the results are real, not theoretical. Across a relatively small number of deals, that strategy has produced more than $300,000 in equity in my own portfolio. I'm not reciting a framework I read about. I'm describing what happened when I applied it with my own money on the line.

I'm not a coach who built a course before they built a portfolio, and I'm not a guru with a Lamborghini in the thumbnail. I'm a working operator who is still buying, still managing, and still executing BRRRR deals in the same secondary Midwest markets that most real estate content completely ignores.

That's exactly what I bring to your deal — a second set of experienced eyes, grounded in what actually works in markets like yours, to help make sure you're making the right decision before you commit.
